## Framework Overview

### Content Quality Dimensions

The framework evaluates content across 6 weighted dimensions:

1. **Content Value & Relevance (25%)**
   - Comprehensive coverage
   - Unique insights
   - User question satisfaction
   - Competitive advantage

2. **Technical SEO Optimization (20%)**
   - Keyword optimization
   - Meta tags
   - Internal/external linking
   - Schema markup

3. **Readability & User Experience (20%)**
   - Reading ease score
   - Formatting and structure
   - Scannability
   - Mobile optimization

4. **E-E-A-T Signals (20%)**
   - Experience (first-hand)
   - Expertise (credentials)
   - Authoritativeness (citations)
   - Trustworthiness (transparency)

5. **Content Freshness & Accuracy (10%)**
   - Current information
   - Dated references
   - Updated best practices
   - Factual accuracy

6. **Engagement & Conversion Optimization (5%)**
   - Clear CTAs
   - User journey
   - Conversion paths
   - Engagement hooks

### Overall Score Calculation

```
Overall Score =
  (Value × 0.25) +
  (SEO × 0.20) +
  (Readability × 0.20) +
  (E-E-A-T × 0.20) +
  (Freshness × 0.10) +
  (Engagement × 0.05)
```

### Publishing Thresholds

| Content Type | Minimum Score | Recommended Score |
|--------------|--------------|-------------------|
| Pillar Content | 8.0/10 | 8.5+/10 |
| Cluster Content | 7.5/10 | 8.0+/10 |
| General Content | 7.0/10 | 7.5+/10 |
| Time-Sensitive | 7.0/10 | 7.5+/10 |

**Universal requirement:** E-E-A-T score must be 7.0+ regardless of overall score.

## Quality Gates Process

Content must pass through 5 quality gates:

### Gate 1: Pre-Writing
**Purpose:** Ensure proper planning
**Key Requirements:**
- Approved content brief
- Keyword research complete
- Competitor analysis done
- Outline approved

**Gate keeper:** SEO Content Planner

---

### Gate 2: First Draft
**Purpose:** Minimum standards met
**Key Requirements:**
- Word count target met (±20%)
- Flesch score 55+
- E-E-A-T signals present
- Overall score 6.0+

**Gate keeper:** Content Writer (self-assessment)

---

### Gate 3: Editorial
**Purpose:** Refine to publishing standards
**Key Requirements:**
- Facts verified, sources cited
- E-E-A-T score 7.0+
- Technical SEO complete
- Overall score 7.0+

**Gate keepers:** Editor + SEO Meta Optimizer

---

### Gate 4: Final Approval
**Purpose:** All standards met
**Key Requirements:**
- Overall score 7.0+ (8.0+ for pillar)
- All checklists 100% complete
- Legal/brand review passed
- All approvals obtained

**Gate keepers:** SEO Content Auditor, Editor, SEO Meta Optimizer, Content Director

---

### Gate 5: Post-Publishing
**Purpose:** Verify live performance
**Key Requirements:**
- No technical errors (2 hours)
- Indexed in Google (7 days)
- Performance meets benchmarks (30 days)

**Gate keeper:** SEO Content Auditor

## E-E-A-T Framework Summary

### Experience (First-Hand Knowledge)
**Signals:**
- First-person language ("we," "our experience")
- Specific real-world examples
- Process documentation
- Case studies with results
- Lessons learned

**Minimum:** At least 2 clear experience signals per article

---

### Expertise (Demonstrated Knowledge)
**Signals:**
- Author bio with credentials
- Professional licenses/certifications
- Years of experience stated
- Technical depth
- Proper terminology usage

**Minimum:** Author credentials visible, expertise demonstrated

---

### Authoritativeness (Recognized Source)
**Signals:**
- Citations to gov/peak bodies
- Links to legislation
- Awards/recognition
- Social proof
- Industry affiliations

**Minimum:** 3+ authoritative citations

---

### Trustworthiness (Transparency)
**Signals:**
- All claims sourced
- Legal disclaimers present
- Contact info accessible
- HTTPS/privacy policy
- Balanced perspective

**Minimum:** Disclaimers + sources + contact info

## Common Content Issues & Fixes

### Issue: Low Content Value Score (5-6/10)
**Symptoms:** Thin content, rehashed information, no unique angle
**Quick Fixes:**
- Add 2-3 specific examples from experience
- Compare to top 3 competitors, add missing information
- Include case study or detailed workflow
- Add FAQ section addressing user questions
- Increase depth by 20-30%

**Effort:** 2-4 hours

---

### Issue: Weak E-E-A-T Score (<7.0/10)
**Symptoms:** Generic content, no credentials, no sources
**Quick Fixes:**
- Add/enhance author bio with credentials
- Include first-person experience statements
- Cite 3-5 authoritative sources
- Add case study or specific example
- Include legal disclaimer if applicable
- Link to NSW Fair Trading/legislation

**Effort:** 1-2 hours

---

### Issue: Poor Readability Score (<7.0/10)
**Symptoms:** Long sentences, dense paragraphs, low Flesch score
**Quick Fixes:**
- Break paragraphs into 2-4 sentences
- Shorten sentences (aim for 15-20 words)
- Add H2 subheadings every 300-500 words
- Convert text to bullet points (3+ items)
- Add visuals (images, tables, diagrams)
- Simplify language (replace complex words)

**Effort:** 1-3 hours

---

### Issue: Technical SEO Gaps
**Symptoms:** Poor rankings, low CTR, not indexed
**Quick Fixes:**
- Optimize meta title (50-60 chars, keyword at start)
- Rewrite meta description (150-160 chars, CTA)
- Add 3-5 internal links with descriptive anchors
- Implement schema markup (Article, FAQ)
- Add alt text to all images
- Verify canonical tag
- Submit to Google Search Console

**Effort:** 30-60 minutes

## How to Use This Framework

### For Content Writers
1. **Before writing:** Review Quick Reference guide
2. **While writing:** Follow E-E-A-T checklist
3. **After writing:** Complete self-audit using Worksheet

1. **On receipt:** Verify Gate 2 passed

1. **Pre-publishing:** Complete full Worksheet audit
2. **Calculate score:** Use scoring calculator
