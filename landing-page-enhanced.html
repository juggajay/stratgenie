Frequently asked questions

Is StrataGenie compatible with Strata Hub?
Yes! StrataGenie is specifically designed for NSW Strata Hub. We track your reporting windows, validate all required fields, and generate JSON exports ready for lodgement.
Do we still need a strata manager?
Many small schemes (2-20 lots) successfully self-manage with StrataGenie. For larger schemes, we complement professional managers by handling routine compliance.
Who owns our data?
You do. Your scheme data belongs to your owners corporation. Export anytime, and we never sell or share your data with third parties.
How secure is the platform?
All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use Australian-based cloud infrastructure with strict access controls and regular security audits.
