# SEO Content Planner Framework - Quick Reference Card

Fast lookup guide for common framework tasks.

---

## Framework at a Glance

```
TOPIC CLUSTER
├─ PILLAR PAGE (2,500-5,000 words)
│  └─ Comprehensive guide to main topic
│
├─ SPOKES (4-6 x 1,500-2,500 words)
│  ├─ Detailed how-to guides
│  ├─ Best practices articles
│  └─ Process/methodology deep dives
│
└─ SUPPORTING CONTENT
   ├─ FAQ page (10-15 questions)
   ├─ Glossary (15-30 terms)
   ├─ Comparison content
   ├─ Case studies
   └─ Tool/resource lists
```

## Search Intent Quick Guide

### Informational ("What is", "How does")
- **Content**: Guides, overviews, how-tos, educational
- **Audience**: Early awareness, learning
- **Tone**: Educational, comprehensive
- **Examples**: Pillar pages, detailed spokes

### Commercial Investigation ("Best", "vs", "Reviews")
- **Content**: Comparisons, best practices, reviews
- **Audience**: Evaluation stage, comparing options
- **Tone**: Analytical, comparative
- **Examples**: Comparison articles, best practices spokes

### Transactional ("Buy", "Sign up", "Download")
- **Content**: Product pages, pricing, signup pages
- **Audience**: Ready to convert/purchase
- **Tone**: Action-focused, persuasive
- **Examples**: Tool recommendations, specific how-tos

### Navigational (Brand + term, "best [category]")
- **Content**: Branded content, landing pages
- **Audience**: Know what they want, finding it
- **Tone**: Brand-focused, clear positioning
- **Examples**: Brand pages, product hub

## Keyword Density Guidelines

- **Primary Keyword**: 0.8-1.5% of total words
- **Secondary Keywords**: Naturally throughout (no target %)
- **LSI Keywords**: 20-30 semantic variations spread naturally
- **Entity Mentions**: 3-5 related entities per article

**Formula**: (Total Keyword Mentions ÷ Total Words) × 100

**Example**: 12 mentions ÷ 800 words = 1.5% (good)

## E-E-A-T Signals Checklist

**Experience**: First-hand examples, case studies, personal use cases
**Expertise**: Credentials, technical accuracy, industry knowledge
**Authority**: External citations, brand mentions, expert quotes
**Trust**: Privacy policy reference, security badges, author bio, contact info

Minimum needed: 3-4 signals per article

---

## Internal Linking Best Practices

- **Minimum**: 3 internal links per article
- **Optimal**: 5-8 internal links per article
- **Anchor Text**: Contextual, descriptive (include keywords where natural)
- **Target Pages**: Back to pillar, to related spokes, to glossary
- **Placement**: Naturally within content (not forced)
- **Pattern**: Hub-spoke topology (pillar central, spokes link back)

---

## Featured Snippet Opportunity Checklist

**Paragraph Snippets**: Answer a question directly (40-60 words)
**List Snippets**: Provide ranked list (5-10 items, 50-60 words each)
**Table Snippets**: Create comparison table (complete, clear headers)
**Video Snippets**: Embed relevant video

**Query Type**: Question-based keywords ("How to", "What is", "Best way to")

## Common Keyword Density Mistakes

| Mistake | Example | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| **Over-stuffing** | Use keyword 20x in 800 words (2.5%) | Use 6-12 times (0.8-1.5%) |
| **Unnatural placement** | "Keyword is a keyword and keyword does keyword things" | Use variations and natural language |
| **Ignoring LSI** | Only use primary keyword repeatedly | Mix in semantic variations |
| **Missing secondaries** | Only target 1 keyword | Target 3-5 secondary keywords |

## Quick Metric Targets

**Ranking Position**: #1-5 within 3-6 months

**Time-on-Page**: 2+ minutes for long-form content
**Bounce Rate**: <60% for informational content
**Internal CTR**: 15%+ of page readers click internal links

## Content Consolidation Checklist

When to consolidate two articles:
- [ ] Same primary keyword target
- [ ] Very similar content structure
- [ ] Both underperforming (low traffic)
- [ ] Conflicting/confusing for users
- [ ] Outdated content + newer article covers same topic

**Process**:
1. Merge into higher-ranking article
2. 301 redirect old URL to new
3. Update internal links pointing to old
4. Submit to Search Console

## Red Flags to Watch

**Stop & Review If**:
- [ ] Same keyword ranking for 2+ articles (cannibalization)
- [ ] Content not ranking after 3 months (quality/depth issue)
- [ ] Bounce rate >70% (relevance or UX issue)
- [ ] E-E-A-T signals absent (credibility issue)
- [ ] Internal links not working (user experience issue)
- [ ] Competitor outranks with weaker content (refresh needed)
- [ ] Cluster stuck at "pillar only" (execution delay)
