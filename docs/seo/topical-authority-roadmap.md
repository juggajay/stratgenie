# Topical Authority Roadmap: Strata Management

12-month strategic plan for establishing topical authority in strata/body corporate management.

---

## Executive Summary

**Goal:** Establish Strata as the #1 authoritative resource for Australian strata/body corporate management within 12 months.

**Target Domains:**
- AGM compliance
- Financial management
- Legal compliance
- Committee operations
- Property maintenance

**Key Metrics:**
- 150+ authoritative articles published
- Top 3 rankings for 50+ core keywords
- Domain authority increase to 45+
- 100+ quality backlinks from industry sources

## Risk Mitigation

### Content Quality Risks
**Risk:** Content lacks depth or authority
**Mitigation:**
- Mandatory technical review for YMYL content
- Author credentialing requirements enforced
- Quarterly content audits
- User feedback loops

### Regulatory Change Risks
**Risk:** Law changes make content outdated
**Mitigation:**
- Monitoring system for legislative changes
- Rapid update protocol (48-hour response)
- Clear "Last Updated" dates on all content
- Quarterly regulatory reviews

### Competition Risks
**Risk:** Established players dominate SERPs
**Mitigation:**
- Target long-tail keywords initially
- Differentiate with superior depth and E-E-A-T
- Build partnerships for link velocity
- Focus on user experience and engagement

### Resource Risks
**Risk:** Insufficient writers or expertise
**Mitigation:**
- Build network of expert contributors early
- Create detailed content briefs and templates
- Invest in training for internal team
- Budget for external expert reviews
