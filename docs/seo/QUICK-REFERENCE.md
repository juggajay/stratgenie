# E-E-A-T Quick Reference Card

Fast reference for content creators and reviewers.

---

## Pre-Flight Checklist (Before Writing)

✓ I am qualified to write on this topic (credentials verified)
✓ I have reviewed existing content on this topic
✓ I know my target keywords
✓ I have identified 5+ authoritative sources
✓ I understand if this is YMYL content (requires extra care)

---

## Writing Checklist

### Must-Have Elements

**Every Article Must Include:**

1. **Author byline** with credentials
2. **Publication date** and last updated date
3. **3-5 primary source citations** (legislation, government sites)
4. **Practical examples** from real experience
5. **Disclaimer** if YMYL content
6. **Clear structure** with H2/H3 headings
7. **Internal links** to related articles (3-5 minimum)
8. **Call to action** (what should reader do next?)

---

## Source Quality Hierarchy

**✅ ALWAYS USE (Tier 1):**
- Australian legislation (legislation.gov.au)
- Government regulatory bodies
- Licensed professional associations (SCA)
- Court judgments

**✅ GOOD TO USE (Tier 2):**
- Peer-reviewed journals
- Industry research reports
- Professional publications
- Government statistics

**⚠️ USE SPARINGLY (Tier 3):**
- Reputable news sites
- Industry news
- Expert blogs (with credentials)

**❌ NEVER USE:**
- Anonymous content
- User-generated content
- Competitor sites (except for comparison)
- Unverified social media

---

## YMYL Content Requirements

If your article covers **legal, financial, or compliance** topics:

**MANDATORY:**
- [ ] Written or reviewed by credentialed expert
- [ ] Legal/financial disclaimer prominent
- [ ] All facts verified against primary sources
- [ ] Jurisdiction clearly specified
- [ ] Technical reviewer sign-off obtained
- [ ] "Last regulatory check" date included

**Disclaimer Templates:**

**Legal:** "This information is general in nature and should not be considered legal advice. Strata laws vary by state and territory. Always consult with a licensed strata manager or legal professional for advice specific to your scheme."

**Financial:** "This information is provided for educational purposes and should not be considered financial advice. Consult with a qualified accountant or financial advisor for advice specific to your body corporate's financial situation."

## E-E-A-T Quick Score

Rate your article 1-10 in each area (target: 8+ for each):

**Experience** (Do I demonstrate hands-on knowledge?)
- Real-world examples included
- Practical tips from experience
- Common pitfalls identified
- Step-by-step guidance provided

**Expertise** (Do I prove my qualifications?)
- Author credentials displayed
- Technical accuracy verified
- Comprehensive topic coverage
- Specialized terminology used appropriately

**Authoritativeness** (Is content well-sourced?)
- 3+ primary sources cited
- Links to official legislation
- Industry association references
- Related internal content linked

**Trustworthiness** (Will readers trust this?)
- Clear disclaimers where needed
- Transparent about limitations
- Current information (dates shown)
- Professional presentation

**Total Score:** ___/40 (Target: 32+)

## Citation Format

**In-text citation:**
```
The NSW Act requires AGMs within 2 months[^1].
```

**Reference at end:**
```
[^1]: Strata Schemes Management Act 2015 No 50, Section 18(1).
Available: [URL]. Accessed: [Date].
```

**Minimum citations per article:**
- Beginner: 3-5
- Intermediate: 5-8
- Advanced: 8-12

## Schema Markup Checklist

Every article must have:
- [ ] Article schema with author
- [ ] Publication dates (published & modified)
- [ ] Publisher information
- [ ] ReviewedBy schema (if technically reviewed)

For specific content types, add:
- [ ] FAQPage schema for Q&A sections
- [ ] HowTo schema for step-by-step guides

Test at: [Google Rich Results Test](https://search.google.com/test/rich-results)

## Internal Linking Rules

**Every article must link to:**
1. Its pillar page (1 link)
2. Related articles in same cluster (2-3 links)
3. Related articles in other clusters (1-2 links)

**Anchor text should be:**
- Descriptive (not "click here")
- Natural in context
- Keyword-relevant

**Example:**
❌ "Click here for more information"
✅ "Learn more about AGM notice requirements"

## Pre-Publication Checklist

**Content Quality:**
- [ ] Spell-checked and grammar-checked
- [ ] Read aloud for flow
- [ ] Headings follow logical hierarchy (H2 → H3)
- [ ] Paragraphs are short (3-4 sentences max)
- [ ] Bullet points used for lists

**E-E-A-T Signals:**
- [ ] Author byline with credentials
- [ ] Publication/update dates
- [ ] Sources cited properly
- [ ] Disclaimer included (if YMYL)
- [ ] Technical review completed (if YMYL)

**SEO Elements:**
- [ ] Title includes target keyword
- [ ] Meta description written (150-160 chars)
- [ ] URL slug is clean and descriptive
- [ ] Featured image selected
- [ ] Internal links added (3-5 minimum)
- [ ] Schema markup implemented

**Technical:**
- [ ] All links work (no 404s)
- [ ] Images have alt text
- [ ] Mobile preview checked
- [ ] Load time acceptable (<3 seconds)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

**❌ Don't:**
- Write YMYL content without proper credentials
- Use anonymous or low-quality sources
- Copy competitor content
- Forget to update "last reviewed" dates
- Skip disclaimer on legal/financial content
- Use generic examples ("Scheme A had a problem...")
- Write in first person for authoritative content
- Over-optimize for keywords (write naturally)

**✅ Do:**
- Show credentials prominently
- Link to primary sources
- Create original content
- Keep regulatory references current
- Use clear disclaimers
- Provide specific examples (with permission/anonymization)
- Write in clear, professional third person
- Focus on helping the reader

## Emergency Updates (Regulatory Changes)

**If legislation changes affecting your content:**

**Within 24 hours:**
- [ ] Flag affected articles
- [ ] Notify content team
- [ ] Begin research on changes

**Within 48 hours:**
- [ ] Update affected content
- [ ] Update citations
- [ ] Update "last reviewed" date
- [ ] Add update notification badge

**Within 1 week:**
- [ ] Publish summary article on changes
- [ ] Email newsletter subscribers
- [ ] Update related articles

## Remember

**E-E-A-T is not optional.** It's the foundation of SEO success for YMYL content.

**Credentials matter.** Show them. Prove them. Update them.

**Sources matter.** Primary sources only for facts. Always link. Always cite.

**Transparency matters.** Clear disclaimers. Honest limitations. Visible dates.

**Consistency matters.** Every article. Every time. No exceptions.
