Design Principles

The rules that make this theme cohesive and premium.

🎨 Cyan is THE brand color

90% of accents should be cyan. All primary buttons, links, and focus states use cyan.

🌟 Restraint creates premium

Not every element needs color. Use neutral backgrounds and let cyan draw the eye to actions.

🔴 Semantic colors only

Colors have meaning. Red = danger, Amber = warning, Green = success. Nothing else.

👁 Visual hierarchy

White text for primary content, slate-300 for secondary, slate-400 for muted/meta.
